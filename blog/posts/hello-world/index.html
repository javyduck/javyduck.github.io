Why This Blog (and What Posts Can Contain)

Why this blog

I spend most of my time doing research on LLM safety, and somewhere along the way I started rebuilding my mathematics from first principles — actual chapters, with proofs, written slowly. This blog is where those notes live, along with occasional thoughts that don’t fit anywhere else.

Posts are organized by topic (math, research, musings) and by time; pick whichever view you like on the home page.

What posts can contain

Posts are written in Markdown or converted from LaTeX. Math renders through MathJax, inline like \(e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0\) or displayed:

\[ \gcd(a,b) = \min\,\{\, ax + by \;\mid\; x, y \in \Z,\; ax + by > 0 \,\}. \]

Code blocks work too:

def gcd(a, b):
    while b:
        a, b = b, a % b
    return a
